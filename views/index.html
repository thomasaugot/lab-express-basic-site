Charles de Gaulle     1959 - 1969

Charles de Gaulle, in full Charles André Joseph Marie
de Gaulle, (born November 22, 1890, Lille, France —
died November 9, 1970, Colombey-les-deux-Églises),
French soldier, writer, statesman, and architect of
France’s Fifth Republic.


"A true leader always keeps an element of surprise up his sleeve, which others cannot grasp but which keeps his public excited and breathless. —Charles de Gaulle"
